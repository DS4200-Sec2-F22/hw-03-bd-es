hw-03: Interactive Webpage, and Marks & Channels

Marks

  • Areas
    • This is a good choice because areas on a map represent different states in a clear and easy-to-understand fashion, as you can easily understand which areas represent which states.

Channels

  • Color (hue)
    • Color(Hue) - The results are blue for democratic and red for republican. This is a good choice because differing hues are a perfect way to represent the separate identities that are voting parties. Both blue and red stand out from each other very well, so the differences in results are easily noticed.

Colormap(s)

  • Categorical
    • Categorical - The red states represent states that voted conservative, and the blue states are democratic voters. This is a good choice because the red and blue hues are commonly connotated with the conservative and democratic parties, so they can be easily understood. As mentioned before, it also allows for the two categories to be easily distinguished from one another.

Marks

  • Points
    • This is a good choice because each point is an individually batted ball from the 2015 season

Channels

  • Color (Hue and Luminance)
    • This is a good choice because the different hues are easily differentiated from each other, and good/bad scoring values can be easily noticed. The luminance(darkness) of the marks also represents hits that have higher (orange) and lower (purple) scoring values.
  • Vertical and Horizontal Position
    • The vertical position represents the launch angle, and the horizontal position represents exit velocity. This is a good choice because the closer to the middle right the points are, the better the contact with the ball is.

Colormap(s)

  • Divergent
    • The better scoring values can be easily distinguished from the others as dark orange, while the lower scoring values are darker purple. The median range is white, while the 'no data' representation is gray. This is a good choice because the two extremes stand out, and the median is not the same color as 'no data'.
